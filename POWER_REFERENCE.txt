================================================================================
  2. POWER TYPE REFERENCE
================================================================================

  neoorigins:active_aoe_effect
    Applies a status effect to all entities in a radius. Configurable effect,
    amplifier, duration, radius, and cooldown. Used for crowd-control abilities.

  neoorigins:active_bolt
    Fires a damaging projectile bolt. Configurable speed and cooldown.

  neoorigins:active_dash
    Propels the player forward in a dash. Configurable power (velocity),
    cooldown, and whether vertical movement is allowed.

  neoorigins:active_fireball
    Shoots a fireball projectile. Configurable speed and cooldown.

  neoorigins:active_phase
    Allows the player to phase through solid blocks. Configurable max depth,
    cooldown, and hunger cost per use.

  neoorigins:active_place_block
    Places a specific block at the targeted location. Configurable block ID,
    max placement distance, and cooldown.

  neoorigins:active_teleport
    Teleports the player to a target location within range. Configurable range,
    cooldown, and targeting mode.

  neoorigins:attribute_modifier
    Modifies a player attribute (e.g., armor, speed, health, attack damage,
    knockback resistance). Configurable attribute, amount, and operation
    (add_value or add_multiplied_base).

  neoorigins:biome_buff
    Applies a status effect while the player is in specific biomes. Configurable
    biome tag, effect, and amplifier.

  neoorigins:break_speed_modifier
    Multiplies block-breaking speed. Optionally restricted to a block tag.
    Configurable multiplier.

  neoorigins:conditional
    Wraps another power with a condition. The inner power only activates when
    the condition is met (e.g., "climbing").

  neoorigins:crop_growth_accelerator
    Accelerates crop growth in a radius around the player. Configurable radius,
    tick interval, and growths per interval.

  neoorigins:crop_harvest_bonus
    Grants extra drops when harvesting crops. Configurable extra drop count.

  neoorigins:damage_in_biome
    Deals continuous damage while in specified biomes. Configurable biome tag
    and damage per second.

  neoorigins:damage_in_daylight
    Deals continuous damage while exposed to sunlight. Configurable damage per
    second and whether the player ignites.

  neoorigins:effect_immunity
    Grants immunity to specific status effects. Configurable list of effects.

  neoorigins:entity_group
    Assigns the player to an entity group (e.g., undead, arthropod). Affects
    interactions with enchantments and potions.

  neoorigins:elytra_boost
    Provides a boost while using elytra flight.

  neoorigins:flight
    Grants creative-style free flight.

  neoorigins:hunger_drain_modifier
    Multiplies the rate at which hunger depletes. Values above 1.0 increase
    drain; below 1.0 decrease it.

  neoorigins:item_magnetism
    Magnetically attracts dropped items toward the player from a distance.

  neoorigins:mobs_ignore_player
    Causes specified mob types to passively ignore the player. If entity_types
    is empty or omitted, all hostile mobs are affected.

  neoorigins:modify_damage
    Multiplies incoming (or outgoing) damage. Can target a specific damage type
    (e.g., drown, fire) or apply to all damage.

  neoorigins:natural_regen_modifier
    Multiplies the rate of natural health regeneration. Values below 1.0 slow
    regen; above 1.0 speed it up.

  neoorigins:night_vision
    Grants permanent night vision. No configuration needed.

  neoorigins:no_mob_spawns_nearby
    Prevents hostile mob spawns within a configurable radius around the player.

  neoorigins:phantom_form
    Toggleable spectral form with optional invisibility and no-gravity flight.

  neoorigins:prevent_action
    Prevents a specific action or damage type from affecting the player. Known
    actions: fall_damage, fire, drown, eye_damage, freeze, SPRINT_FOOD.

  neoorigins:projectile_immunity
    Grants immunity to specific projectile types (e.g., arrows, spectral
    arrows).

  neoorigins:regen_in_fluid
    Regenerates health while submerged in a specified fluid (water or lava).
    Configurable HP per second.

  neoorigins:scare_entities
    Causes nearby entities to flee from the player. Optionally restricted to
    specific entity types; if omitted, all nearby mobs are affected.

  neoorigins:shadow_orb
    Summons orbiting shadow orbs that protect the player. Configurable max orb
    count, orbit radius, cooldown, and tick interval.

  neoorigins:size_scaling
    Scales the player's model size. Values below 1.0 shrink; above 1.0 enlarge.

  neoorigins:starting_equipment
    Grants an item on first spawn. Configurable item, enchantments, and a
    grant ID to prevent re-granting.

  neoorigins:status_effect
    Applies a permanent status effect. Configurable effect, amplifier, ambient
    flag, and particle visibility.

  neoorigins:thorns_aura
    Reflects a percentage of incoming melee damage back to the attacker.
    Configurable return ratio.

  neoorigins:underwater_mining_speed
    Removes the underwater mining speed penalty, allowing full mining speed
    while submerged.

  neoorigins:wall_climbing
    Allows the player to climb vertical surfaces like a spider.

  neoorigins:water_breathing
    Grants the ability to breathe underwater indefinitely.
